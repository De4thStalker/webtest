Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a tropical island south of India, famous for its beaches, ancient history, and flavorful cuisine.

Sri Lanka is a popular tourist destination due to its natural beauty, historical sites, and vibrant culture. Tourists flock to its beaches, ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, and the cultural city of Kandy.
